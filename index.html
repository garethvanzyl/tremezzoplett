Garden Route coastal living

Your seaside escape in Plettenberg Bay

Welcome to Tremezzo, a luxurious Italian-style villa on the sands of Central Beach. With panoramic sea and mountain views, direct beach access, and stylish, family-friendly comfort, this is your dream coastal getaway in the Western Cape.

A home designed for rest, beauty & togetherness

Tremezzo sleeps up to 10 guests across five beautifully appointed en-suite bedrooms. Unwind in two ocean-facing lounges, soak up the sun on the rooftop deck, or retreat to the enclosed pool area for a refreshing dip.

At Tremezzo Plett, you can enjoy

  • Direct beach access to a beautiful cove
  • Five en-suite bedrooms over upstairs and downstairs levels
  • Sea-facing main bedroom with enclosed glass patio
  • Gourmet kitchen with pantry, walk-in cold room and appliance area
  • Two spacious lounges, each with a fireplace
  • Rooftop deck and beach-facing outdoor deck
  • Fully enclosed pool area
  • High-speed fibre internet and battery back-up power
  • A fully secured property
  • A staff-accommodation suite for a nanny or chef

Situated on the most desired stretch of Central Beach

Tremezzo Plett offers unrivalled access to Central Beach, with sweeping views of the Indian Ocean and Outeniqua Mountains. Walk out the door and onto the sand, then explore coastal paths, nature, restaurants and the best of Plettenberg Bay from your doorstep.
